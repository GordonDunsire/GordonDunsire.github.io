Map from deprecated to published RDA classes

This map relates the deprecated URIs in rdvocab.info to the published URIs for the RDA Classes in rdaregistry.info.

The map is given in a terse triple language (ttl) serialization.



@prefix owl: <http://www.w3.org/2002/07/owl#> .
@prefix rdac: <http://rdaregistry.info/Elements/c/> .
@prefix xrdaFRBR: <http://rdvocab.info/uri/schema/FRBRentitiesRDA/> .
#
# Map from deprecated RDA classes to published RDA classes
# 24 May 2014
#
rdac:C10001 owl:sameAs xrdaFRBR:Work .
rdac:C10002 owl:sameAs xrdaFRBR:Agent .
rdac:C10003 owl:sameAs xrdaFRBR:Item .
rdac:C10004 owl:sameAs xrdaFRBR:Person .
rdac:C10005 owl:sameAs xrdaFRBR:CorporateBody .
rdac:C10006 owl:sameAs xrdaFRBR:Expression .
rdac:C10007 owl:sameAs xrdaFRBR:Manifestation .
rdac:C10008 owl:sameAs xrdaFRBR:Family .
